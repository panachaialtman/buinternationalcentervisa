Before you request university documents

  1. Check your permission-to-stay date

    Use the latest Immigration stamp in your passport. Do not rely only on the visa sticker or e-Visa validity period.

  2. Check your passport expiry date

    An extension cannot normally be granted beyond the passport expiry date. Renewing the passport first may avoid a shortened extension.

  3. Confirm your academic status

    Check whether you are continuing study, in your final semester, awaiting graduation approval, or already graduated.

  4. Open the correct detailed page

    Each route has different academic evidence and eligibility screening.

Not sure whether you are graduating?

Undergraduate students can review their graduation eligibility through the BU graduation checklist system. The system result supports your discussion with the International Center but does not itself guarantee immigration eligibility.
