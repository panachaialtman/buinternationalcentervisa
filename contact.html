Information to include

Complete information allows the team to identify the correct record and urgency without repeatedly requesting basic details.

  • Identity Full name, student ID, nationality, and only the last four digits of the passport number in the initial message.
  • Immigration deadline The exact “permitted to stay until” date and 90-day due date when relevant.
  • Current situation Whether you are studying, in the final semester, awaiting graduation, graduated, outside Thailand, or planning travel.
  • What happened A short chronological explanation with exact dates.
  • Relevant evidence Clear images of the passport biodata page, latest Immigration stamp, receipt, or official notice when requested.
  • The outcome you need State the exact question or action required.

    Before visiting

    • Bring the original passport.
    • Bring the latest registration or payment receipt when relevant.
    • Save digital copies of documents that may only be downloadable once.
    • Bring accommodation evidence requested for your case.
    • Check university closure announcements.
    • Dress appropriately when an Immigration visit is part of the process.

    Privacy and security

    Protect passport information

    Do not post passport images or personal immigration documents in public comments or social-media threads. Use the official private channels and send only the documents required for your case.

    Other university matters

    The International Center can help route international students, but another office may make the final decision on:

    • Course registration, academic records, and transcripts
    • Graduation approval and degree documents
    • Tuition invoices, receipts, and payment records
    • Programme-specific academic matters
    • Housing contracts and property-owner documents
