When should I start my visa extension?

Contact the International Center at least one month before the permission-to-stay date in your passport. Begin earlier when the passport will expire soon or your academic status requires additional review.

What is the difference between visa validity and permission to stay?

Visa validity concerns when a visa may be used for entry. Permission to stay is the date stamped by Immigration showing how long you may remain in Thailand. For an extension deadline, use the latest “permitted to stay until” date.

Can I extend at an Immigration office in Bangkok?

Bangkok University-supported student extensions are normally processed according to the International Center’s instructions at Pathum Thani Immigration. Do not change offices without checking first.

How many credits do I need?

Bachelor’s students are normally expected to register at least 9 credits per semester, while graduate students are normally expected to register at least 6 credits. Final-semester and exceptional cases require individual academic and immigration review.

Can a student with withdrawn or failed courses extend?

Eligibility depends on the complete academic record. A semester with no successfully completed credits, withdrawal from study, or serious academic problems may prevent the university from supporting an extension.

How long can ED Plus last after graduation?

An eligible graduate may be considered for up to one additional year under ED Plus, but the result cannot normally extend beyond passport expiry and remains subject to Immigration approval.

Do ED Plus holders need a re-entry permit?

The official ED Plus framework includes an exemption from obtaining a separate re-entry permit. Rely on the exemption only when Immigration has granted ED Plus and the passport contains the applicable exemption wording.

Can I work with a student visa or ED Plus?

Student immigration status does not by itself authorize employment. Work must comply with the applicable Thai work-authorization requirements.

When can I complete the 90-day report?

The standard reporting window is 15 days before through 7 days after the due date. Online submission should be attempted within the 15-day advance period, before the due date.

Where should I complete my 90-day report?

Use the Immigration office responsible for your actual residential address. This may be different from Pathum Thani Immigration, where BU-supported student extensions are normally handled.

Does leaving Thailand reset the 90-day count?

Yes. After re-entry, the count begins again from the latest arrival date. Re-entry does not extend the separate permission-to-stay date.

Does the 90-day report extend my visa?

No. TM.47 only reports your address. It does not extend your stay or replace an extension application.

Why was my online 90-day report rejected?

Common reasons include mismatched information, a new passport, submission outside the online window, no compatible previous record, or an unresolved previous application. Report in person when the system instructs you to do so.

I moved to a new residence. What should happen?

The householder, property owner, possessor, or accommodation provider is responsible for TM.30 notification when required. Moving to a new address generally requires the accommodation record to be updated. Keep proof for future immigration applications.

My passport expires before the expected extension end date. What happens?

If you do not renew the passport, Immigration may only grant an extension until the passport expiry date. Renewing first may allow consideration of the period normally available under the relevant policy.

I received a new passport. What should I do?

Update the BU student record and contact Immigration to transfer the relevant entry, extension, and re-entry information. Keep both passports when the old passport remains available.

My passport was lost or stolen. What should I do?

Obtain a police report, contact your embassy for a replacement or emergency passport, notify the International Center, and contact Immigration to reconstruct or transfer your immigration record.

Can I travel while my extension is being prepared?

Travel may affect the current stay, re-entry requirement, appointment, or document validity. Check with the International Center before making travel plans.

Can another person collect my university documents?

A representative may collect documents only when the International Center permits. The representative normally needs the original passport and any authorization evidence requested for that case.

Which tuition or registration receipt should I use?

Use the latest receipt requested for the current semester or visa application. Save the file in an accessible location because some registration receipts may only be printable once.

Can I pay tuition to the “222” account?

No. Use the official BU payment channel at bupay.bu.ac.th. If online payment is not possible, contact the International Center at least one week before the payment due date.

What accommodation document is required?

For Pathum Thani accommodation, Immigration may request a currently valid rental contract and a copy of the property owner’s Thai ID. For accommodation in another province, TM.30 evidence is normally required. Case-specific documents may be added.

What photograph should I prepare?

A recent 4 × 6 cm photograph taken within the past six months is commonly requested for Immigration applications. Follow the current checklist issued for your case.

Does a BU supporting letter guarantee approval?

No. BU confirms eligible student information and prepares supporting documents. The responsible Thai embassy, consulate, or Immigration office makes the final decision.

What should I wear to Immigration?

Dress appropriately in a smart-casual style. Immigration is part of the Royal Thai Police; remain polite and respectful and avoid clothing that is excessively casual or revealing.

Where is the visa-service office?

Visa and immigration assistance is provided by the International Center at C6 Building, 3rd Floor, Bangkok University Rangsit Campus. Check appointment messages because other International Center services may use different service points.

Which office handles registration, grades, or graduation records?

The International Center assists with international-student visa services, but registration, transcripts, grades, graduation approval, tuition, and programme matters may require the Registrar, Finance office, academic faculty, or Graduate School. The International Center can help route the question.

What information should I include when contacting the International Center?

Include your full name, student ID, nationality, passport number ending, current permission-to-stay date, a concise description of the problem, and clear images of the relevant passport stamps when appropriate.

When is a case urgent?

Treat the case as urgent when the permission to stay has already expired, will expire within seven days, the passport is lost, Immigration has issued an unexpected instruction, or planned travel may invalidate the current status.
