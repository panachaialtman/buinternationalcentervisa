How to use this website

Three important distinctions

BU supporting documents

The university prepares documents for eligible students. This does not guarantee approval.

Authority decision

The responsible Thai embassy, consulate, or Immigration office makes the final decision.

Your legal deadline

Always follow the “permitted to stay until” date in your passport—not only the visa sticker validity.

Important terminology

Visa validity concerns entry using a visa. Permission to stay is the date stamped by Immigration showing how long you may remain in Thailand. An extension of stay is requested inside Thailand.
